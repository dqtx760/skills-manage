OpenAI (2015–Present)

Mission: Safe Artificial Intelligence

Founded as a non-profit research company to ensure that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity. Musk co-founded OpenAI with Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, and Wojciech Zaremba.

  • Concerned about existential risk from unregulated AI development
  • Published groundbreaking research in reinforcement learning (GPT series, Dota 2)
  • Stepped down from board in 2018 due to conflict of interest with Tesla's AI work for autonomous driving
  • Currently criticizes OpenAI's shift to closed-source, profit-driven model

"With a high-bandwidth brain-machine interface, we can actually go along for the ride. We can get symbiosis with AI, and ultimately create a world where AI is aligned with human values."

The Boring Company (2016–Present)

Started as a joke about traffic congestion in Los Angeles, became a real company.

  • Goal: Reduce urban traffic through tunnel networks
  • Developing faster, cheaper tunneling technology
  • Projects: Las Vegas Convention Center Loop
  • Vision: 3D networks of tunnels for electric vehicles

Problem

When roads get crowded, you can go down — build multiple layers of tunnels to bypass surface traffic.
